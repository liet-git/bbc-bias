A pharmacist inside the Nasser hospital who was among those evacuated by Israeli forces has described a scene of panic during today's raid. Rawan Al-Mughrabi told BBC Arabic a large group of people were sheltering inside the complex and "many people were harmed, and others returned to the hospital". "There was a state of panic that made people stand on top of each other and scream," she says . "As soon as we left the hospital gate and reached the checkpoints, the entire hospital and departments were stormed by police dogs, and while we were standing at the checkpoints, many people were arrested. Most of the medical cases were evacuated from the hospital, and only the very critical cases remained and so did the hospital director.
