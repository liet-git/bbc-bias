T-E-X-T-S-T-A-R-T. We have a response now to the operation in the Nasser hospital from Hamas-run health ministry in Gaza. Spokesman Dr Ashraf al-Qudra says the IDF "forced the remaining displaced persons and families of medical teams to forcibly move from Nasser Medical Complex at dawn today under bombardment and threats". He says the Israeli army has asked hospital staff to transfer all patients, including those in intensive care, to a separate building in the hospital complex. Al-Qudra accuses the IDF of demolishing the southern wall of the hospital with a bulldozer, as well as targeting ambulance headquarters, the tents of displaced people and mass graves at the hospital.
