France's foreign minister has expressed her sorrow at the news of the death of Elia Toledano, 28, a French citizen who was taken hostage by Hamas in the 7 October attack on Israel. Catherine Colonna said "we share the pain of his family and loved ones" after Toledano's body was recovered by the Israeli military.
