Portugal has summoned the Iranian ambassador, the foreign ministry said in a statement. Lisbon says the action was to reiterate Portugal's condemnation of Tehran's attack on Israel on Saturday and to demand the release of a Portuguese-flagged container ship, MSC Aries, seized by Iran on 13 April. "The foreign ministry will await the results of this formal measure and evaluate any additional steps," the statement added. T-E-X-T-E-N-D.
