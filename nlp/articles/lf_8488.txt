The Israel Defense Forces (IDF) has given an update on its operations in Gaza, posting footage on social media of what they say are raids on schools in Gaza City that are hiding Hamas members. The footage shows soldiers shooting from behind the rubble of destroyed buildings of what it says is the Rimal neighbourhood close to the centre of the city. The IDF said they had "eliminated" Hamas operatives in the fighting and that others they found in the schools, "surrendered and were arrested by the forces". The IDF added that they had directed an aircraft to destroy a building in the Jabaliya area, where they say they had detected "the movement of a number of Hamas terrorists on the roof". They said shots were fired at them before they attacked the building. In addition to the raids in Gaza City, the IDF gave details of operations in Khan Yunis , to the south of the Strip. They said fighters had found "many weapons and underground infrastructure" following raids on apartments in the area. T-E-X-T-E-N-D.
