A medical charity says it is imploring EU leaders to prevent “further abhorrent bloodshed” in Gaza ahead of a key meeting in Brussels. Doctors Without Borders (MSF), a charity that provides humanitarian medical assistance, writes on X , formerly Twitter, that they ask each EU leader to do “whatever is in your power to protect the lives and dignity of Palestinians trapped in Gaza”. They call on the EU’s heads of state to exercise their “unique position" to convince Israel to stop "the indiscriminate bombing of the Gaza Strip and protect people facing unimaginable horror”.
