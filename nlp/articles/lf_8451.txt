France has joined the calls for an "immediate and durable" ceasefire in the Israel-Gaza war. The country's Foreign Minister, Catherine Colonna said Paris is "deeply concerned" over the situation in the territory, the AFP news agency reports. "Too many civilians are being killed," Colonna added, after she met her Israeli counterpart Eli Cohen in Tel Aviv. She stressed that the victims of Hamas's 7 October attack should also not be forgotten. T-E-X-T-E-N-D.
