The president of the court, who will deliver the ruling, is Judge Joan E. Donoghue. Donoghue is an American lawyer who was elected to the ICJ in 2010. Before that she was a legal adviser in the US State Department in the Obama administration. She is the second woman to hold the post of president and the third American.
