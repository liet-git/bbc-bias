Following recent strikes carried out by the Israel Defense Forces (IDF), BBC Verify has obtained satellite images showing some of the destruction in Khan Younis – a city in southern Gaza. The before and after photos below – taken on 5 and 7 January – show a number of what appear to be flattened multi-storey buildings towards the north east of the city. The area is next to a major road which runs south to Rafah. Khan Younis has increasingly become a focal point for IDF operations. As well as ongoing bombardment, IDF spokesman Avichay Adraee says troops have moved “deep” into the city . T-E-X-T-E-N-D.
