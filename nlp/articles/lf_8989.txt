The Israel Defense Forces (IDF) has shared video of what it claims is a tunnel found in a mosque in Khan Younis, in central Gaza. The video, taken by a drone flying through the ruined remains of the mosque, appears to show an entrance to a tunnel in a small room. Separately, the IDF published a video of what it claims to be another tunnel used by Hamas, found near the Rantisi Hospital in Gaza. The BBC has not been able to independently verify the material.
