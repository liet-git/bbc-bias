Ben Kersten, from Jewish Voice for Peace at UCLA, has spoken to the BBC from the protest. He says formations of officers are grouped on a number of sides around the encampment. Kersten says police have begun detaining people around the edges and at the entrance of the encampment. He also says police are setting off flash bangs - an explosive designed to disorient people - over the camp. Law enforcement is yet to confirm whether any arrests have been made. T-E-X-T-E-N-D.
