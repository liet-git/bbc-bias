In a new statement, the Israel Defense Forces (IDF) says it has apprehended a number of suspects in the Nasser hospital operation. It repeats that the goal of the "precise and limited operation" if to find Hamas operatives, included those suspected of involvement in the 7 October attacks on Israel. The IDF says it officially contacted the director of the hospital on Tuesday, calling for the immediate cessation of all Hamas "terrorist activity from within the hospital and the immediate evacuation of all Hamas terrorists from within". It adds that the operational activity around the hospital complex is ongoing. T-E-X-T-E-N-D.
