Hezbollah has now confirmed the killing of Wissam Tawil - a senior commander in the group's elite Radwan force. Hezbollah does not detail the cause of Tawil's death, but earlier Reuters news agency cited three security sources as saying he was killed in an Israeli airstrike on a car in southern Lebanon. Wissam Tawil was the deputy head of a unit within Hezbollah's elite Radwan force, Reuters reports. T-E-X-T-E-N-D.
