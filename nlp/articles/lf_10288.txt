Meanwhile family members of hostages held by Hamas in Gaza have joined other demonstrators outside the US embassy in Tel Aviv. They have gathered behind an empty coffin with red paint on their hands, calling for an end to the war and the immediate release of all hostages. One holds a placard urging US president Joe Biden to use his influence to help stop the war. Biden warned Israeli PM Benjamin Netanyahu against launching an offensive in Rafah in a phone call on Monday, after previously describing an invasion of Rafah as a "red line". Some 128 hostages remain unaccounted for, with at least 34 presumed dead following the Hamas attacks on Israel on 7 October. T-E-X-T-E-N-D.
