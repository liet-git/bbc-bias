The Polish foreign ministry is seeking official confirmation from Israel about reports a Pole was among the aid workers killed during a suspected Israeli strike on Gaza. As we reported earlier , Mayor of Przemyśl Wojciech Bakun identified the Polish aid worker as Damian Soból. But, moments ago Polish foreign ministry spokesman Paweł Wroński told a news briefing the ministry had received reports, including a statement from World Central Kitchen (WCK) seven of its team, including a Polish citizen, had been killed in the strike. Officials say they are seeking official confirmation from the Israeli government about the victim's identity. Colleagues of Soból have posted comments on social media that he had taken part in delivering aid to Ukrainian refugees following Russia's invasion of Ukraine in February 2022. T-E-X-T-E-N-D.
