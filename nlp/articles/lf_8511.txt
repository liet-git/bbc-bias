T-E-X-T-S-T-A-R-T. The United Nations Security Council has called for an independent investigation into "reports of the discovery of mass graves" near two hospitals in Gaza. Hundreds of bodies, including women, children and older people, have allegedly been found in and around Gaza's Nasser and al-Shifa medical facilities, the UN Security Council has said in a statement . It calls for independent investigations to start immediately and to "be allowed unimpeded access to all locations of mass graves". The UN Security Council members also reaffirmed in the statement the importance of protecting "civilians and civilian objects" under international law. T-E-X-T-E-N-D.
