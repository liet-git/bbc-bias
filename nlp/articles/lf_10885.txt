On 7 October, the BBC’s Middle East bureau chief Jo Floto received a text from a correspondent in Gaza saying: “They’ve started a war. ”. In the six months since then, Floto has co-ordinated the BBC’s coverage of this unprecedented conflict from his office in Jerusalem. In an episode of The Global Story podcast, he tells Katya Adler what it has been like for his team, professionally and personally, and how he sees the war unfolding in the months to come.
