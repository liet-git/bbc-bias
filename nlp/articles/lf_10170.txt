The Israeli military has now released the name of the third and final hostage it's admitted to accidentally killing, after misidentifying the group as a "threat". Alon Shamriz was abducted from Kibbutz Kfar Aza on 7 October, officials say, the day Hamas crossed the border into Israel and killed 1,200 people. In a brief update, the Israel Defense Forces (IDF) says Shamriz's family have given permission for his name to be published alongside that of Yotam Haim and Samer Talalka - the other two hostages killed. "The IDF expresses deep sorrow for the incident and shares in the grief of the families," it adds. T-E-X-T-E-N-D.
