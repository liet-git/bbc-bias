T-E-X-T-S-T-A-R-T. Al Jazeera is reporting that two of its journalists have been wounded in the southern Gaza Strip. According to Reuters, Wael al-Dahdouh and Samer Abu Daqqa were wounded by a missile fired from a drone near Haifa school in the centre of Khan Younis. Al-Dahdouh previously lost his wife, teenage son, young daughter and a grandson in Israeli strikes in the Gaza Strip. 63 journalists and media workers have been killed due to the fighting in Gaza in 7 October, according to the Committee to Protect Journalists.
