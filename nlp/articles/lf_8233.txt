 F-N-S-T-A-R-T. 8233 F-N-E-N-D.  D-A-T-E-S-T-A-R-T. 2024-02-21 D-A-T-E-E-N-D.  T-I-T-L-E-S-T-A-R-T. Sunak says 'more to come' on tackling knife crime. T-I-T-L-E-E-N-D.  T-E-X-T-S-T-A-R-T. Labour MP for Liverpool Riverside Kim Johnson asks about the government's plans for tackling "the scourge of knife crime". Sunak says there are plans in place that are working, but there is more to come. He says legislation is coming to increase criminal sentences for knife crime, as well as a ban on so-called zombie knives. He says he hopes Johnson and Labour will support the legislation when it is brought before the House. T-E-X-T-E-N-D.
