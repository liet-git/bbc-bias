Israeli strikes targeted the Al-Maghazi refugee camp in southern Gaza Strip once again overnight. The camp lies to the east of what Israeli army calls "an extended humanitarian zone" around Deir al-Balah. Here are some images from the camp in the aftermath of the strikes: A Palestinian woman looks at what is left of her home. A Palestinian child walks on the rubble of her family house. A man tries to retrieve personal items. Much of Al-Maghazi camp, which had been experiencing overcrowding after people fled bombardments further north, now lies in ruins. T-E-X-T-E-N-D.
