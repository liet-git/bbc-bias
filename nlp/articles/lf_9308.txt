Israel’s Ministry of Defense says it has received "the 200th cargo plane carrying military equipment for the Israel Defense Forces (IDF)". A press release by the ministry says over 10,000 tons of military equipment have been delivered to Israel since the start of the war, including armoured vehicles, weapons, personal protective equipment, medical supplies and ammunition. It adds that efforts to obtain the equipment are being led by the ministry’s US Procurement Mission. The United States is Israel’s largest military backer, providing about US$3.8bn (£3bn) of defence aid per year. T-E-X-T-E-N-D.
