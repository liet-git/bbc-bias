For those of you just joining or waking up, a total of 33 MPs have signed a motion of no confidence in the Commons Speaker, Sir Lindsay Hoyle. The motion was proposed by Tory MP William Wragg in reaction to Hoyle's handling of the Gaza ceasefire vote.
