 F-N-S-T-A-R-T. 10259 F-N-E-N-D.  D-A-T-E-S-T-A-R-T. 2023-12-14 D-A-T-E-E-N-D.  T-I-T-L-E-S-T-A-R-T. IDF pauses fighting in Rafah for 'humanitarian purposes' T-I-T-L-E-E-N-D.  T-E-X-T-S-T-A-R-T. Earlier, the IDF announced it was pausing fighting in the al-Salam neighbourhood of Rafah, to give residents the chance to get water and food and other much-needed items. It lasted for four hours and ended at 14:00 local time (12:00 GMT). Rafah lies on Gaza's southern border with Egypt, where many Palestinians fled when the war began on 7 October. The crossing is also where aid into Gaza is coming in from. T-E-X-T-E-N-D.
