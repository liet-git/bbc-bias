Earlier we reported on comments by a World Health Organization representative, who said there are just 11 partially functioning hospitals in Gaza – down from 36 at the start of the war. Richard Peeperkorn also stressed the need to expand bed capacity in Gaza, and to “restore the functionality of the system”. He added that he was concerned about the present situation, and that he hoped that “incredibly vulnerable health infrastructure will be left intact and that we can actually expand on that”.
