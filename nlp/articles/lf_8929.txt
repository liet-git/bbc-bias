An independent transnational Palestinian think tank says without a political solution there is no end to the conflict between Hamas and Israel. As we wait for the UN's resolution vote later today, Zena Agha tells the BBC that it feels it is "a little too late". She adds there may even be some "push back" from Russia and other countries. "The US and others who are seeking to water down the discourse are really swimming against the current," she says. Zena adds the US has the power and "considerable leverage". T-E-X-T-E-N-D.
