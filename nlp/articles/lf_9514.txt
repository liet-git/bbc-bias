Kirby is asked by a journalist whether the Israeli strike on the aid convoy was a mistake. The journalist tells Kirby that Israeli Ha'aretz newspaper has reported that there was not one strike but three. It said the aid workers got out of their vehicle after the first strike, removed the wounded and tried to move to another vehicle which was struck. Then there was a third strike when they moved to another vehicle, he adds. Kirby is asked how the second and third strikes could be a mistake, to which he says the investigation will look into the military's decision making process. "The IDF know it was their mistake and their error. Let them investigate and we will go from there," Kirby says. T-E-X-T-E-N-D.
