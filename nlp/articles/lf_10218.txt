In an update, the IDF says its special forces have retrieved the man's body during an operation in Gaza. Elia Toledano, 28, was taken hostage at the Supernova music festival on 7 October, alongside his friend Mia Schem who was freed two weeks ago during the temporary pause in fighting. Toledano's body has been returned to Israel, where it was identified by medical officers and police. "The IDF sends the family its heartfelt condolences," a statement says. T-E-X-T-E-N-D.
