T-E-X-T-S-T-A-R-T. The Israeli military has told Gazans to leave the area around the al-Shifa hospital. Located in Gaza City in the north of the Gaza Strip, thousands have already fled since Israel began its operation in October. The hospital is the largest in the region and is being used as a shelter for displaced people, as well as patients. IDF Arabic language spokesman Avichay Adraee said on social media: “A call to all those present and displaced in the al-Rimal neighborhood and in al-Shifa Hospital and its surroundings: In order to maintain your security, you must immediately evacuate the area to the west and then cross al-Rashid (al-Bahr) Street to the south to the humanitarian area in al-Mawasi. ”.
