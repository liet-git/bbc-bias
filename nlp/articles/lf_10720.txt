A UK businessman who has lost five members of his family in bombing in Gaza says he feels "broken" and "destroyed". Dalloul al-Neder, who owns a takeaway in Manchester, says his mother, brother, pregnant sister-in-law and two nieces all died in the strike on their home in Jabalia over a week ago. He says he fears for the rest of his relatives who were injured, including his wife and six-month-old daughter. He knows his daughter was pulled from the rubble but has no more news.
