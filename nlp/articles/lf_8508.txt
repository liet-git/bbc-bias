 F-N-S-T-A-R-T. 8508 F-N-E-N-D.  D-A-T-E-S-T-A-R-T. 2024-05-11 D-A-T-E-E-N-D.  T-I-T-L-E-S-T-A-R-T. 'Face-to-face battles' in eastern Rafah, according to IDF. T-I-T-L-E-E-N-D.  T-E-X-T-S-T-A-R-T. Israel Defense Forces has posted on X, formerly called Twitter , saying that over the past day "fighters of the combat team of the Givati Brigade eliminated a number of terrorists in face-to-face battles" in Rafah. They continue by saying that the brigade have in addition "destroyed terrorist infrastructures established in the area". The IDF post also says that fighters of the 401st Brigade combat team have "located a number of underground shafts and closed another circle of armed terrorists in the area" in co-operation with the air force.
