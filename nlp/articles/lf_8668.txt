T-E-X-T-S-T-A-R-T. Iran has responded to the death of senior Hamas figure Saleh al-Arouri. Foreign Ministry spokesperson Nasser Kanaani says the explosion in Beirut will "undoubtedly ignite another surge in the veins of resistance and the motivation to fight", reports Iranian state media. Kanaani added this will also encourage resistance to fight against Israel, not only in Palestinian territories but also in the region and "among all freedom-seekers worldwide". Iran supports both Hamas and Lebanon's Hezbollah. T-E-X-T-E-N-D.
