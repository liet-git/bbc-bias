The Israel Defence Force (IDF) says it has recovered the bodies of two Israeli soldiers who were taken hostage by Hamas during its 7 October attack on southern Israel. Nik Beizer , 19, was on duty at an IDF base at Erez Crossing, a route between Gaza and Israel, when it was attacked. Ron Scherman , also 19, was abducted while serving as a non-commissioned officer in the Gaza Coordination and Liaison Administration, the IDF said. In a post on X, formerly Twitter, the IDF said: "We share in the grief of the families and will continue to support them. " You can read more about the people taken from Israel on 7 October here .
