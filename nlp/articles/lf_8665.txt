The leader of Hamas, Ismail Haniyeh, has just issued a statement confirming that two senior commanders from its armed wing have been killed in Beirut alongside Saleh al-Arouri. He names the pair of al-Qassam Brigades commanders as Samir Fendi and Azzam al-Aqra, adding that others were killed too, without giving any more names or numbers of the dead. A separate Hamas statement also reported the killings of four other men, taking the total death toll from the attack to seven. In his statement, Haniyeh condemned their "cowardly" killing, as well as accusing Israel of violating Lebanon's sovereignty and escalating the ongoing conflict. T-E-X-T-E-N-D.
