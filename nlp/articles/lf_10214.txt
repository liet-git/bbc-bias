As we've reported, the Israeli military say their special forces have recovered the body of Elia Toledano, 28, who had been held by Hamas in Gaza since the 7 October attacks on southern Israel. Toledano was among a number of people to be seized from the Supernova music festival close to the border with Gaza. No details have been provided on how or when he died, but the military said an "identification procedure" had been carried out by medics, military rabbis and forensic experts. Toledano was at the Supernova festival with friend Mia Schem, 21, who was released on 1 December as part of the temporary truce deal.
