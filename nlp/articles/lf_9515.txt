Kirby is asked if firing a missile at people delivering food and killing them is a violation of international humanitarian law. “The Israelis have already admitted that this was a mistake that they made," Kirby says, reiterating that an investigation is being carried out into what happened. “Let’s not get ahead of that. ”. He says the question presumes that the Israeli strike against aid workers was deliberate and that they knew what they were hitting, to which he adds, “there’s no evidence of that". He says that to date, the State Department has not found any incidents where the Israelis have violated international humanitarian law. T-E-X-T-E-N-D.
