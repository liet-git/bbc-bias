Throughout the last two months food shortages has been a huge issue for civilians as a result of the Israel-Gaza war, with thousands at risk of infection and death. According to humanitarian aid groups there has also been a difficulty in delivering aid due to the intensity of fighting. We've seen crowds chasing trucks just to get something to eat at the Rafah crossing, and in Rafah city hundreds have gathered at food distribution sites. Hundreds gathered in Rafah to receive food cooked by a charity kitchen. A food and water shortage is putting many at risk. Many people do not have the resources they need to live. T-E-X-T-E-N-D.
