T-E-X-T-S-T-A-R-T. The Israeli army has continued to bomb Jabalia refugee camp in the north of the Gaza Strip, as Israeli tanks advance towards the centre of the camp. Local journalist Hossam Shabat, who reports on the north of Gaza, said in a recorded message shared on social media: “We are surrounded by tanks that are firing smoke and fire bombs at a shelter on Abu Zaitoun Street in the Jabalia camp, which is home to 15,000 displaced people. “For more than three hours, the violent bombardment has not stopped, and ambulances cannot get here: All hospitals in northern Gaza are out of service. “Fires broke out in many buildings in the camp, terrifying thousands of displaced people who fled their homes to seek shelter in UN schools. ”.
