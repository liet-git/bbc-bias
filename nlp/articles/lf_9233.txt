Families of hostages have been gathering in Karmiel, northern Israel, as the country observes the Jewish festival Hanukkah. Idit Ohel, mother of Alon Ohel, 22, who is believed to be being held in Gaza, and her family were among those who lit candles in the city this evening. Israel says 138 hostages seized by Hamas gunmen on 7 October are still in captivity in Gaza - 110 people were freed during the week-long truce which has now broken down. T-E-X-T-E-N-D.
