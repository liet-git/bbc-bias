Iran's Foreign Minister Hossein Amirabdollahian said Iran would give an immediate response if there was a significant attack from Israel. In an interview with NBC News on Friday, Amirabdollahian said if Israel acts against Iran's interests, his country's "next response will be immediate and will be at the maximum level". Amirabdollahian downplayed the presumed Israeli drone attack on Isfahan in central Iran, questioning whether Israel was involved. He said the mini-drones were "like toys our children play with".
