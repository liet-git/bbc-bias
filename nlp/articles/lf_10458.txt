Gaza's Hamas-run health ministry says the number of people killed as they waited to receive aid has risen to 112. Spokesperson Dr Ashraf al-Qudra adds that 760 people were injured. He says that, according to witnesses, the bodies of some victims are yet to be recovered from the Nabulsi roundabout, on the south-western edge of Gaza City, suggesting the number of casualties could rise further.
