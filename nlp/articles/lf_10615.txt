Family members of Americans held hostage by Hamas have praised the Biden administration after a meeting with the US president at the White House earlier. Speaking in a press conference afterwards, Jonathan Dekel-Chen says it was a "terrific meeting". "We all came away feeling that. . . we could have no better friend in Washington," he says. Meanwhile Liz Naftali - whose relative Abigail has been released by Hamas - says the families were hoping for "a Christmas miracle", and the return of more hostages. "We would love all of our loved ones to come back and be with us for Christmas," she says.
