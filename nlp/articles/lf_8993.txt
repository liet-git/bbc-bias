More than 500 Israeli troops have been killed since the war against Hamas began on 7 October, Israel's military has said. It added more than 160 IDF soldiers had been killed since its ground offensive inside Gaza started in late October. Some 900 soldiers have been wounded since that operation began - with troops engaging in close combat with Hamas fighters. Israel says it will not stop its retaliatory campaign in Gaza until it destroys Hamas. At least 1,200 people were killed when Hamas attacked Israel on 7 October, and about 240 others were taken hostage.
